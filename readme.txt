Mobile Woocommerce app templates, connect your store in minutes and have a complete native IOS or Android app for your customers on the App store.

== Description ==

Ready-made templates for your WooCommerce store, create a mobile IOS and Android app with little effort. We are trusted by thousands of companies worldwide to provide the best platform for your store.

Our template supports the latest WooCommerce (3.5+)
Label StoreMax is an app template for WooCommerce stores. You can create an app for your store in minutes and then upload it to the App store or Google play store.

== Installation ==

Upload the WooSingal WooCommerce app plugin to your blog, Activate it and start using our custom plugin to power your WooCommerce store.

1, 2, 3: You're done!
